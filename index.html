Find your next purrfect companion. All breeds shown are currently available!

Siamese

Known for their striking blue almond-shaped eyes and distinctive color points. They are highly vocal and social companions.

Maine Coon

Often referred to as the "gentle giants" of the cat world. They have shaggy coats and bushy tails, making them look like miniature lions.

Persian

Famous for their flat faces and long, luxurious coats. They are sweet, quiet, and enjoy a calm and predictable environment.

Ragdoll

True to their name, they go limp when picked up. They are known for their beautiful blue eyes and docile, affectionate nature.

Sphynx

A hairless breed known for its wrinkled skin and large ears. They crave warmth and attention, often cuddling under blankets.
